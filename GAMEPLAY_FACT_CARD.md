# Gameplay Fact Card - Firefly Forest Trail

- Core screen: portrait SpriteKit night forest play field with a movable lantern and drifting fireflies.
- Player action: drag the lantern through fireflies and tap/select Gold, Green, or Blue lantern light.
- Main rule: a firefly is captured only when its color matches the active lantern light.
- Feedback: wrong color captures prompt the player to switch to the correct light.
- Goals: capture a target number of fireflies before the countdown timer ends.
- Progression: 50 local forest areas, unlocked level progress stored on device.
- Scoring: score, combo count, rare golden fireflies, area clear and area failed outcomes.
- Settings: Music and Sound Effects toggles stored in NSUserDefaults.
- Privacy model: offline single-player, no login, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, no IAP.
