Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Firefly Forest Trail is an offline single-player iPhone game. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Information Collection

Firefly Forest Trail does not collect personal information. The app does not include account registration, social login, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party purchase services.

Local Game Data

The app may store gameplay progress, unlocked areas, scores, music settings, and sound effects settings locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Children's Privacy

Firefly Forest Trail is suitable for all ages. Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Internet, Ads, and Purchases

  • No internet connection is required for gameplay.
  • No advertisements are included.
  • No in-app purchases are included.
  • No user tracking is included.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.
